## Troubleshooting

### Prayer Times Seem Wrong

**Step 1: Check Confidence Score**
- Low confidence? Refresh times
- Check internet connection
- Verify GPS is enabled

**Step 2: Verify Location**
- Is the city name correct?
- Is GPS accuracy good (< 100m)?
- Try moving to open area for better GPS

**Step 3: Try Different Method**
- Go to Prayer Settings
- Try different calculation methods
- Compare with local mosque times

**Step 4: Use Fine-Tuning**
- Add/subtract minutes as needed
- Match your local mosque exactly
- Save adjustments

### Low Confidence Score

**Possible Causes**:
- Poor internet connection
- GPS accuracy issues
- API temporarily unavailable
- Location services disabled

**Solutions**:
1. Enable high-accuracy GPS in device settings
2. Connect to stable Wi-Fi or cellular data
3. Pull down to refresh prayer times
4. Wait a few minutes and try again
5. Check location permissions

### Times Not Updating

**Quick Fixes**:
1. Pull down on home screen to refresh
2. Go to Prayer Settings and refresh
3. Check location services are enabled
4. Verify app has location permission
5. Restart the app

### GPS Accuracy Poor

**Tips for Better GPS**:
- Move to area with clear view of sky
- Avoid tall buildings and dense trees
- Wait 30-60 seconds for GPS to lock
- Enable high-accuracy mode in device settings
- Restart location services

## FAQ

### Q: How accurate are the prayer times?
**A**: With high confidence (80-100%), times are accurate to within 1-2 minutes.

### Q: Do I need internet connection?
**A**: Internet helps for best accuracy, but the app works offline using cached times and local calculations.

### Q: Why does the confidence score change?
**A**: It depends on internet speed, GPS accuracy, and how well sources agree.

### Q: Can I trust low confidence times?
**A**: They're still calculated correctly, but you may want to refresh or verify with your local mosque.

### Q: What if I'm in a remote area?
**A**: The app will use local astronomical calculations, which are still very accurate.

### Q: Do times update automatically?
**A**: Yes! Times update at midnight, when you travel >5km, or when you manually refresh.

### Q: Can I set custom prayer times?
**A**: Not directly, but you can use fine-tuning to adjust each prayer by minutes.

### Q: What's the best calculation method?
**A**: For North America (US/Canada), use ISNA. For other regions, choose the method your local mosque uses.

### Q: Why do I see different sources?
**A**: The app uses the most reliable source available. It may switch between sources for best accuracy.

### Q: Does this use a lot of data?
**A**: No, prayer time data is very small (< 1 KB per day).
