Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

Muslim Space respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use the Muslim Space mobile application.

Information We Collect

Muslim Space may collect the following information:

  • Location data: Used to provide accurate prayer times, Qibla direction, and location-based features.
  • Email address: Used for account creation, authentication, and user support.
  • Usage and device data: Such as device identifiers and interaction data to improve app performance and reliability.

How We Use Your Information

Information collected is used solely to operate, maintain, and improve Muslim Space, including providing core religious features, improving user experience, and ensuring app functionality.

Advertising

Muslim Space uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect and use data such as device identifiers in accordance with Google’s Privacy Policy. Users may see personalized or non-personalized ads depending on their device settings.

Data Sharing

Muslim Space does not sell personal data. Information is shared only with trusted third-party services necessary to operate the app, such as authentication and advertising providers.

Children’s Privacy

Muslim Space does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. If such information is discovered, it will be deleted promptly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
